Energy Meter App

An energy meter app to visualize electrical readings from the sensor. It also has user logins to view all their respective electrical readings.

The engineering students have their sensor which displays readings into its LCD. The problem is readings can be observed only at the sensor's LCD and cannot be observed from afar. So my solution is using a web server that collects and stores sensor readings. An ESP01 wifi sensor to send readings to the webserver. And an android mobile app to display readings into the user's smartphone.
